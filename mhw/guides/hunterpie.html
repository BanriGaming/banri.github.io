HunterPie Overlay & Rich Presence

HunterPie is a modern and simple to use overlay with support for Discord Rich Presence for Monster Hunter: World & Rise.

  1. Home
  2. Game Library
  3. Monster Hunter: World
  4. Guides

The things you can do with HunterPie include the following:

  1. Get a brand new player info bar with more details
  2. Damage & DPS Parsing
  3. Detailed Class Helpers
  4. Plugins/Add-Ons that give extra features
  5. Discord Rich Presence, which allows one to see HR/MR, Weapon Currently Equipped, What Locale your in, What your hunting; with health % remaining and quest time. All this can be seen when clicking on a player in discord.
