How to start expedition

To start the expedition, you have two options:
You can start a new game in Expedition mode
Or you can summon the Space Anomaly from any existing save and start the expedition from expedition terminal.
The second option is better, as you can bring resources and technologies with you, or even bring a copy of your starship or multitool, which greatly speeds up expedition progress!

Preparation (Optional)

First of all, if you're a veteran player, you can make the run much easier by bringing some resources with you!

  1. This time you can bring 64 Activated Copper, 99 Atlantideum, 12 wiring looms, and 100 salt, along with Gold, Chromatic metal, magnetized ferrite and Sodium Nitrate.
  2. Everything you bring will be available at the same terminal within the expedition! To retrieve items, you must warp once - this will unlock the space anomaly within the expedition! In other words, the initial mining is inevitable!

Notable things during expedition

Keep in mind these two things:

  1. Don't forget to claim rewards for milestones, as some of them contain plans for the next milestones.
  2. Also, use the refiner if you need to process anything.

The Guide

  1. Once you start the expedition, document plants and animals you find around and mine basic resources such as carbon, oxygen, and so on.
  2. Always identify plants and animals on any planet you visit.
  3. Activate waypoints at points of interest you notice.
  4. Eliminate 6 hazardous flora to complete the "In the Weeds" milestone.
  5. Select "Still They Hunger" milestone and use your scanner to search for the large tree-like structures. They spawn Hungering Tendrils which you must fight. Don't forget to collect the vile spawns they drop. Defeat 5 Hungering Tendrils to complete it.
  6. Reach your starship to finish the "Alone in the Dark" milestone.
  7. Install an advanced mining laser technology. Doing so will complete the "From the Ashes" milestone and let you mine large resource nodes!
  8. Mine condensed carbon and install a personal refiner Mk 2 to complete the "Self Reliance" milestone.
  9. Return to your starship and repair its pulse engine and launch thruster. To get deuterium, refine silicate powder with di-hydrogen.
  10. Leave the planet and go to space to complete the "An Unlikely Escape" milestone.
  11. Find asteroids and destroy 30 of them to get some tritium and finish the "Worlds of Rock and Ice."
  12. Visit Orey XII Humid planet in this system and mine at least 99 activated copper and 99 atlantideum.
  13. Harvest one Heptaploid Wheatâ€”we'll need it later.
  14. Mine one Radiant Shard to complete the "Call of the Void" milestone.
  15. Next, visit Zabet Beta Infested planet.
  16. Select "Stirring the Nest" milestone and collect larval cores from whispering eggs. These will spawn hostile insects; ignore them and collect 9 whispering eggs to finish this milestone.
  17. Collect around 10 storm crystals during the storm on this planet for warp hypercores.
  18. Visit the temperate planet Adlindse and land near the ocean.
  19. Document 16 new plants to complete the "Life, Reimagined" milestone.
  20. Collect 6 crystal sulfides from the ocean to finish the "The Lure of the Deep" milestone and 4 more for future crafts.
  21. Collect 3 ancient bones to finish the "Bones to Dust" milestone.
  22. Visit the space station and check its rooms to complete the "Lights Out" milestone.
  23. Sell healthy wheatblock, ancient bones, and items obtained from the Hungering Tendrils. Don't sell the eye from your ship's storage as you'll need it later.
  24. Buy wiring looms from the trade terminal if you didn't bring them with you.
  25. Refine excess copper and activated copper into chromatic metal and craft 5 microprocessors.
  26. Install a hyperdrive module and fuel it.
  27. Visit Tavievel Fuji Scalding planet.
  28. Select the "The Faintest Echo" milestone and use a signal booster to scan for transmitters. Visit the communication tower and interact with its terminal to finish it.
  29. Repair three multitool components, then repair 3 starship components to finish the "Make Do and Mend" milestone.
  30. Document 24 creatures to complete the "Not Alone" milestone.
  31. Activate the "Abandonment" milestone and use a signal booster to find a damaged ship. Restore it to finish the milestone.
  32. Install hyperdrive modules from the reward on your starship.
  33. Select "Rendezvous 1" milestone and warp toward the marked destination.
  34. Warping the first time completes the "Independent People" milestone.
  35. Summon the space anomaly and retrieve items transferred from your main save.
  36. Warp until you reach the "Rendezvous 1" location to complete this milestone. Collect extra larval cores around the building.
  37. Running 6500 units finishes the "Time on Your Feet" milestone.
  38. Process larval cores into nanites to get 1200 and finish the "Quantum Foam" milestone.
  39. Select "Amidst the Stars" milestone.
  40. Visit the main terminal at the space anomaly, craft and install the mind arc, use it at the terminal, and visit the portal it navigates to.
  41. Interact with the terminal and enter the portal, then watch a cutscene to complete this milestone.
  42. Activate the "Rendezvous 2" milestone and visit the marked location to complete it.
  43. Land on the tallest mountain near the destination to finish the "All That You Survey" milestone.
  44. Select "To Dream of Stars" milestone, activate the pulse engine, and watch a cutscene to finish it.
  45. Visit the space station and buy remaining wiring looms to make it 12.
  46. Install cadmium drive and mine cadmium in a red star system.
  47. Install emeril drive and mine emeril in a green star system.
  48. Install indium drive, activate "It's Alive" milestone, and visit a blue star system it points to.
  49. Find a planet with synthetic fauna, document 2 of them, and complete the milestone.
  50. Activate "Rendezvous 3" milestone and visit the marked location.
  51. Discover 3 ancient ruins with a signal booster to complete "Nameless History."
  52. Activate "Rendezvous 4" milestone and visit the marked location.
  53. Activate "Rendezvous 5" milestone and visit the marked location.
  54. Look at the stars for 60 seconds to finish the "A Still, Small Voice" milestone.
  55. Select "Ghost in the Machine," use a signal booster to find a reality breach, and finish the milestone.
  56. Build a nutrient processor, process Heptaploid Wheat into flour, faecium into wild yeast, and combine them to make dough, process it into bread, and finish "By Bread Alone."
  57. Activate 6 waypoints to finish "Signs to Nowhere."
  58. Visit a planet with a deep ocean, mine 100 salt or synthesize it, build a nautilon, install a high-power sonar, and search for a sunken freighter. Visiting its core completes "Fathoms Below."
  59. Use the reward receiver, activate the pulse drive, follow the questline to locate a derelict freighter, and finish "Dereliction."
  60. Activate "To Dream of Freedom," activate the pulse drive, and watch a cutscene to finish it.
  61. Activate "To Dream of Sleep," activate the pulse drive, and watch a cutscene to finish it. That was the final milestone; claim your Iron Vulture!
