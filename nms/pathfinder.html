Pirate Freighter 4 Square Supercharged

Euclid

  1. Disable Multiplayer in Network Settings before going through Portal.
  2. Set Combat Settings to None before going through the Portal.
  3. Upon arrival, go to space and warp to an adjacent system
  4. Land at the Space Station and Turn combat settings back to Normal before getting out of your ship.
  5. Exit your ship to Create a Restore Point.
  6. Warp back to the target system you arrived at to get the Freighter Battle.
  7. Destroy only the Engines & Big Beam Turrets and then Enter the freighter and check the class via Scanner.
  8. If its a C, B or A class, Reload Restore Point and Repeat steps 6 and 7 until you get S Class.
