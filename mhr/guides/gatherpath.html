Gathering Pathing Guide

A guide to acquire 400K Zenny & 35-50K in appoximately 10-15 minutes.

Zenny/Gathering Path

  1. Start by grabbing the 3rd Wirebug in Area 1 before heading off to the first mining node.
  2. After the 3rd node, drop down the hole into the lower lava caverns to grab the next batch of nodes.
  3. The Orange X marked in the 2nd pathing image is a Gustcrab, I recommend grabbing it and using it to get to the next node. It will respawn on your 3rd round through the map.
  4. When you reach the crawl tunnel, there will be a node on entry, and a wall to climb. When ascended, take the left pathway. There are 4 nodes in here on the Left path.
  5. Teleport to Sub-Camp 2 to continue.
  6. Continue the rest of the path. After final node, Teleport to Main Camp, Enter the tent to deposit materials on entry and immediately exit and begin again.
